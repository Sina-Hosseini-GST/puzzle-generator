Instructions

  • Add any width and height for the maze you want to generate.
  • All the sizes are flexible, you can generate any size you want (Recommended puzzle size is less than 50).
  • The sections with North Part, East Part, West Part and South Part lets you decide what direction the solved paths should use.
  • Small number will have a straight path and larger numbers will have more turns.
  • These directions are optional. You can leave them blank. Recommended numbers are already selected.
  • After filling what you want, click on Generate Maze to see the result of your maze.
  • You can click Download Image to only download the maze.
  • You can click Download Answered Image to download the solution of your maze.
