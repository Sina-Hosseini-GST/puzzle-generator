Instructions

  • Add a heading to your puzzle. This step is optional but recommended.
  • Click Add button to add as many Hints and Answers you want for the puzzle.
  • Click Generate to generate your puzzle.
  • Click Solve to see the solution of your puzzle. You can click Solve again to hide the solution.
  • You can click Download Puzzle to download your puzzle as an image. You can download both solved and unsolved versions of the puzzle based on what you see on your screen.
  • You can also download editable text of your puzzle using Download Text button.
