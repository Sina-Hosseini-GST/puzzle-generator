Maze Generator

The objective of the mazes is to find a path from one end to the other.

You can use this Maze Generator to generate multiple difficulties from easy, medium and hard based on the grid size. You can also generate either square or rectangular grids for these mazes.

Sudoku Generator

The objective of the Sudoku is to fill a grid so that each column and each rowcontains the digits based on the size of the grid.

You can use this Sudoku Generator to generate multiple difficulties and grid sizes. You can generate the following grid sizes:

  • 3x3
  • 6x6
  • 9x9

You can generate the following difficulties for each grid:

  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard
  • Very hard

Crossword Generator

The Crossword Puzzle consists of a grid of squares and blanks into which words crossing vertically and horizontally are written according to clues.

Based on the number of clues, you can generate all kinds of difficulties for this Crossword Generator. You can create the puzzle as easy or as difficult you want.

Word Search Generator [v1]

Word Search consists of the letters of words placed in a grid, which usually has a rectangular or square shape. The objective of the puzzle is to find and mark all the words hidden inside the box based on the clues given.

You can use this Word Search Generator to generate both square and rectangular grids. You have a freedom to choose any grid size you want.

The version 1 of the Word Search Generator circles the words given in the clues.
