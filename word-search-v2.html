Instructions

  • You can add a heading to your puzzle. This step is optional but recommended.
  • You can also change the font of the puzzle. This step is also optional.
  • Choose width and height of the puzzle. The size is flexible. You can create a square or a rectangular puzzle.
  • You can add a list of words for each puzzle.
  • This puzzle generator ignores all the spaces, numbers and special characters so you donâ€™t have to edit the list.
  • The Directions let you the direction of your words from 8 different angles.
  • You can see the highlighted solution of your puzzle after you generate each puzzle.
  • You can save the puzzle as an image. You can download both solved and unsolved versions of the puzzle based on what you see on your screen.
  • You can copy the puzzle as an editable text at the bottom of each puzzle.
  • You can copy the word list in alphabetical order as an editable text at the bottom of each puzzle.
